## Auxiliary Cyber Defense 

Auxiliary Cyber Defense is a forward-thinking cybersecurity startup founded in 2026 in Malawi with a bold and strategic mandate: to defend national infrastructure, government institutions, and critical digital ecosystems against government-backed adversaries, advanced persistent threats (APTs), and emerging cyber warfare tactics. Established at a time when digital transformation is accelerating across Africa, Auxiliary Cyber Defense was created to serve as a resilient shield for Malawi’s sovereign digital space and to strengthen cybersecurity capacity across the region.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to proactively protect and secure national infrastructure, public institutions, and mission-critical systems through advanced threat intelligence, continuous monitoring, incident response, and cutting-edge defensive technologies. We are committed to identifying vulnerabilities before adversaries exploit them, neutralizing cyber threats in real time, and building resilient digital environments capable of withstanding sophisticated attacks.

We aim to empower governments and organizations with actionable cybersecurity intelligence, robust defense frameworks, and locally developed solutions tailored to the unique security landscape of Malawi and the broader African continent.

## Our Vision

Our vision is to become Malawi’s leading authority in national cyber defense and a recognized cybersecurity powerhouse across Africa. We envision a digitally secure nation where government systems, financial institutions, energy grids, telecommunications, and healthcare platforms operate without disruption from hostile cyber actors.

Auxiliary Cyber Defense strives to set the standard for cybersecurity excellence by fostering innovation, developing local cyber talent, and contributing to a safer, more resilient global digital ecosystem.
